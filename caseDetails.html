Brand Situation
Leading prestige skincare “doctor brand” from the USA active in Chinese department stores was struggling to unlock market potential due to issues in channels, marketing, and registration.
SBA Added Value
  • Provided the brand with an active and dedicated local presence
  • Instituted basic business planning processes in distributor: activity grids, promotion planning, new door opening plan, forecasts based on sell out not sell in, etc.
  • Provided global product training to strengthen beauty advisor capabilities
  • Coached and developed local distributor marketing team and led PR events
  • Drove intensive product registration efforts  to increase product portfolio.
Results
Brand retail sales doubled  then doubled again over  the 4 year period of  SBA engagement.
